I am a Ph.D. Student in Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University, working with Anqi (Angie) Liu. Prior to that, I was a Machine Learning Resident at VinAI Research, under the supervision of Toan Tran and Dinh Phung. I completed my B.Eng. in Information Technology from the Elite Program at PTIT.

Research interests

I am interested in developing Unsupervised and Reliable Machine Learning algorithms, while at the same time understanding why they work in high-dimensional statistics. My work often involves Representation Learning, Probabilistic Graphical Models, Bayesian Inference, and its applications in Generalization and Uncertainty problems.
